How to use the Elevation Visualizer

If you've ever wondered what a trip you're planning on taking looks like in terms of elevation, this is the app for you!

  1. Enter your starting address and your destination then press Go
  2. The blue graph represents elevation along your total journey. The x-axis represents the corresponding direction maneuver while the y-axis represents the elevation at the starting point of that maneuver
  3. A list of directions will also be displayed. Clicking one of these directions will display a second graph with an orange line. This graph represents the elevation along that part of the journey
  4. Entering a new set of locations or clicking Clear will reset the app and allow you to start over
